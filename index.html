Our Story

About Pugs & Petals

Pugs & Petals is a passion project born from two things we love most — our hilariously opinionated pugs and the simple joy of being outdoors. What started as a photo account to share Gary's dramatic side-eyes and Brenda's unstoppable enthusiasm has grown into a community of pug lovers, plant parents, and anyone who believes life is better with a squishy face nearby.

We host seasonal meet-ups, pug-friendly garden walks, and pop-up events where four-legged guests are always the VIPs. Whether you're a lifelong pug devotee or just discovering the magic of the breed, you're always welcome here.

Meet the Pugs

Gary's Photo

Gary

The Philosopher 🤔

Gary approaches every situation with a dramatic pause and a deeply considered side-eye. He is suspicious of squirrels, obsessed with afternoon naps, and holds strong opinions on which side of the sofa is objectively superior. Somehow, he is always right.

🌸

Brenda's Photo

Brenda

The Enthusiast 🌸

Brenda sees every stranger as a friend she simply hasn't met yet. She has an unmatched zest for life, an alarming interest in whatever is in your pocket, and a tail that has never once stopped wagging. She is the heart of every room she enters.
